Why it matters

Your money, your future

Managing your finances doesn't have to be complicated. Whether you're just starting out or trying to break a bad habit, a few simple habits can make a huge difference. Budgeting isn't about restricting yourself — it's about being intentional so your money works for you, not against you.

Core tips

6 beginner-friendly budgeting tips

Try the 50/30/20 rule

Split your income: 50% on needs, 30% on wants, 20% on savings. Simple and effective.

Track every expense

Write down what you spend for one month. Awareness is the first step to change.

Build an emergency fund

Aim for 3–6 months of expenses saved. Start with just RM500 if needed.

Automate your savings

Set up an auto-transfer on payday. If you don't see it, you won't spend it.

Pay yourself first

Save before spending. Treat savings like a non-negotiable monthly bill.

Review monthly

Spend 10 minutes at the end of each month reviewing your budget. Adjust as needed.
